# Legacy migration rules

## v4 → v5

Preserve v4 as the Atlas projection. Add:

- profile support;
- understanding session;
- coverage boundary;
- evidence ledger;
- reader contracts;
- Focus visual projection.

Migration cannot determine the current human question or risk boundary. Generated Focus scaffolding remains `UNVERIFIED` until source and task context are re-read.

## v3 → v5

Legacy domains and ordered labels may be preserved as evidence of prior documentation only. Do not invent actors, triggers, guards, states, failures, outcomes, or compensation.

A migrated stream cannot become `VERIFIED` until source/test/schema/config/runtime evidence is captured and the v5 stream, coverage, and understanding gates pass.
